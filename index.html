About Me

Hey there!

I'm Jacob Ross, a professional web developer and designer in Portland, OR. I've received my education from The Art Institute of Portland and I've been playing with web technologies for the past several years. I really enjoy trying to find ways to use CSS instead of exporting images from designs. It's a good challenge.

There are a lot of people are doing incredible things to innovate the web right now and I want to be one of them.
