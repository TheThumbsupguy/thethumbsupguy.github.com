About Me

Hey there! I'm Jacob Ross, a student at the Art Institute of Portland studying web development and design. I've been playing with web technologies for the past four years and cartooning every since I could hold a pencil. I'm really interested in what the web industry has to offer. There are so many awesome people working in this industry making cool stuff and I want to be one of those people. I am really excited about making ridiculously fun and awesome games, sites, apps, and cartoons! My dream is to be able to develop games and to be able to do my own artwork for them. Let's do this! :D
